    3. Red Fish
    4. Blue Fish

    At least one selection is required.
    Up to 4 selections are allowed.

    Possible answers are:
    1. One Byte
    2. Two Bites
    3. Red Light
    4. Green lights

    The user will be presented with the ability to search for: Person.
    The field to return is: Name.
