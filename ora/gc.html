Grants & Contract Services

Coming together to foster the research mission

Moving forward to provide exceptional support for university-wide research

Regardless of which Indiana University campus you call home, the Office of Research Administration will be with you every step of the way during your journey through a successful research project.  We will work with you throughout the life of your project -- from proposal submission to award closeout.  Our office provides the following services:

  • proposal preparation assistance and consultation
  • budget reviews
  • submission of proposals to funding agencies
  • negotiate and accept awards from external sponsors
  • set up sponsored accounts
  • provide training on pre and post award topics
  • monitor expenses for financial compliance
  • prepare and submit invoices
  • collect income
  • provide audit support
  • close out accounts
