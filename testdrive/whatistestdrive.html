Development for the Kuali Test Drive has focused on the basic functionality for a solid introduction to the Kuali Financial System (KFS). It includes the following modules:

Chart of Accounts – fundamental Chart business objects to characterize financial accounts, including "eDocs" (electronic documents) to approve database entries

General Ledger – "Scrubber" and "Poster" processes to validate and post to the General Ledger, plus reports such as Nightly Out and Balance Inquiry

Financial Transactions – eDocs for initiating and approving common business transactions:

  • Cash Receipts
  • Transfer of Funds
  • Journal Voucher
  • Disbursement Voucher

Standard Reports – sample financial reports, such as Account Status, Account Transactions, and trial balances from which you can create external financial reports.

Nervous System – Kuali infrastructure, including object lookups, database abstraction, and application constants

Kuali Enterprise Workflow (KEW) – Full integration with KEW infrastructure that automatically routes eDocs online according to prescribed business rules

Inquiry and Maintenance – User screens to create and change basic Kuali objects and database entries.

Start at the Kuali Test Drive menu and explore how KFS eDocs can initiate business transactions and route them for approval.
